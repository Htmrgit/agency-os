AGENCYPRO - Universal Business OS
AGENCYPRO is a high-performance, enterprise-grade single-file web application designed to empower agencies and small businesses. By consolidating CRM, Asset Management, and Daily Operations into one seamless interface, it eliminates the need for complex multi-tool setups and expensive subscriptions.
Comprehensive Overview:
AGENCYPRO operates entirely within the browser, utilizing advanced Client-Side storage (LocalStorage) to ensure your data remains private, secure, and accessible 100% offline. The UI is crafted with a premium Dark Mode aesthetic featuring a sophisticated Orange and Green color palette for maximum productivity and visual comfort.
Key Features:
* Enterprise CRM Pipeline: Master your sales with detailed prospect dossiers, automated Lead ID generation, and a chronological conversation timeline to track every client touchpoint.
* AI & Asset Library: A high-density grid-based repository for prompts, templates, and procedures. Features include one-click "Fast Copy" functionality and custom category labeling.
* Daily Ops Register: Modern task management with automatic timestamps, category filtering (Client, Internal, Urgent), and dedicated "Progress Update" logs for every entry.
* Team Brainstorming Board: A visual sticky-note style interface for quick drafts and creative ideas, categorized for easy retrieval.
* Universal Data Vault: Maintain total control over your data. Export your entire business ecosystem into a single JSON file for backups or workspace migration.
* Privacy First Architecture: No backend, no databases, and no tracking. Your business intelligence stays in your browser.
